How to PLAY (PC)

Pocky moves in the direction of your cursor - drag your mouse accordingly! To launch him initially, left-click your mouse.

The objective is to move Pocky along by making him bounce on clouds and advance further up into the sky. Your score is measured by how high you can go, plus the number of bonus goodies you feed him along the way.

Can you help Pocky reach the very top of the sky for his ultimate dream prize?
